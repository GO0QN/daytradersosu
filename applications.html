Treasurer Application

What you’ll own

  • Budgeting for programs and events.
  • Reimbursements and vendor payments.
  • Tracking dues, fundraising, and sponsorships.
  • Clear, periodic financial reporting.

What to include

  • Relevant experience (treasurer roles, budgets, audits).
  • Tools you’re comfortable with (Sheets/Excel, QuickBooks, etc.).
  • Availability (weekly hours) and any links (resume, portfolio).
Commitment: ~3–6 hrs/week
Works with: President, Events, Sponsors

We’re looking for someone organized, detail-oriented, and friendly with spreadsheets.

Guest Speaker Application

Ideal speakers

  • Traders with a teachable edge or repeatable process.
  • Professionals with market, risk, or analytics insights.
  • Builders (tools, data, platforms) relevant to intraday trading.

What to include

  • Talk topic and 3–5 bullet outline.
  • Short bio and links (site, LinkedIn, slides, examples).
  • Preferred dates/times and AV needs.
Format: 30–45 min + Q&A
Audience: OSU students, all levels

Help members level up by sharing your experience and mistakes — not just highlights.

Standards

Board of Traders Application

What you’ll do

  • Set and uphold process standards for members.
  • Lead post-trade reviews and journal audits.
  • Mentor members on risk, sizing, and execution.
  • Contribute dashboards, templates, or research.

What to include

  • Experience (years, markets, styles) and risk framework.
  • Primary strategies and examples of process.
  • Links (journals, dashboards, repos) if available.
  • Weekly availability.
Commitment: ~3–5 hrs/week
Focus: Risk, review, accountability

We’re after thoughtful, process-first traders who enjoy teaching and holding a standard.
