About OSU Day Traders

We’re a student-led community focused on building consistent, disciplined trading processes. Members get hands-on practice, structured playbooks, and accountability partners.

What We Do

  • Daily watchlists, levels, and reviews
  • Risk management and journaling
  • Guest speakers and workshops
  • Collaborative research
