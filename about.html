About OSU Day Traders

We’re a student-led community focused on building consistent, disciplined trading processes. Members get hands-on practice, risk management frameworks, and a place to share playbooks and debriefs. Whether you’re brand new or experienced, you’ll find structure, accountability, and people who take the craft seriously.

What We Do

  • Daily watchlists, levels, and post-market debriefs
  • Risk management and journaling systems
  • Guest talks, workshops, and challenge weeks
  • Collaborative research on strategy edges

Leadership

President

Sets vision and standards, oversees operations, and represents the org with partners and the university.

Vice President

Drives execution of initiatives, manages internal teams, and ensures consistency across programs.

Public Relations

Leads community outreach, social/content, and partnerships. Owns messaging and external communications.

Secretary

Keeps docs, meeting notes, and schedules organized. Maintains member records and compliance.

Treasurer

Budgets events, manages reimbursements, and tracks dues/sponsorships with transparent reporting.

Education Lead

Designs workshops, bootcamps, and study tracks (risk, journaling, setups, platform basics).

Events Coordinator

Plans speakers, challenge weeks, and collabs. Handles logistics and day-of execution.

Risk & Analytics

Builds dashboards, journaling templates, and post-trade reviews to enforce process and edge.
