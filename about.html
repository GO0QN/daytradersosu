About OSU Day Traders

We’re a student-led community focused on building consistent, disciplined trading processes. Members get hands-on practice, structured playbooks, and accountability partners. Whether you’re brand new or experienced, you’ll find people who take the craft seriously.

What We Do

  • Daily watchlists, levels, and post-market reviews
  • Risk management and journaling systems
  • Guest speakers, workshops, and challenge weeks
  • Collaborative strategy research
