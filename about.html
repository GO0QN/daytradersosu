About OSU Day Traders

We’re a student-led community focused on building consistent, disciplined trading processes. Our mission is to help Buckeyes learn markets the right way: risk-first, rules-based, and data-driven.

Our Pillars

  • Risk Management: position sizing, stop placement, and journaling.
  • Edge Development: technicals, market structure, statistics.
  • Playbook & Review: backtesting, screenshots, and iteration.
  • Community: accountability groups and mentorship.
