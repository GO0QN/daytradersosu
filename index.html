What we teach

Foundations

Market Basics & Platforms

Order types, market structure, timeframes, and platform setup so beginners can place clean, intentional trades.

  • Order flow, liquidity, and levels
  • Paper trading and journaling 101
Strategies

High-probability Setups

Opening drive, pullback to VWAP, liquidity sweep, and range-break retests with clear rules and invalidations.

  • Entry triggers and invalidations
  • Pre/post-trade checklists
Risk

Position Sizing & Process

How much to risk, where to place stops, and how to scale. Survival math over hero trades.

  • Fixed-fraction vs ATR sizing
  • Daily loss limits & review cadence
Practice

Strategy Lab & Reviews

Backtests, live paper sessions, and weekly debriefs to turn notes into muscle memory.

  • Group backtests and dashboards
  • Journal audits and accountability
Options

Options Basics & Risk

Greeks, premium decay, and how to avoid dumb lottery-ticket trades. Simple, hedge-aware plays only.

  • Defined-risk vs. undefined-risk
  • When NOT to trade options
Tools

Data, Journals & Dashboards

Build a personal edge database: logs, tags, and stats that actually change behavior.

  • Review templates and KPIs
  • Automation ideas and exports
